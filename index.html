Welcome to Grade 6

Explore your math learning outcomes

Ratios & Proportions

Understanding concepts of ratio and using ratio reasoning to solve problems.

  • Ratios and rates
  • Ratio tables & graphs
  • Compare ratios and solve rate problems
  • Percent concepts & calculation

The Number System

Fluency with multiplication and division of rational numbers and fractions.

  • Divide fractions by fractions
  • Multi-digit division & decimal operations
  • Common factors and multiples
  • Positive & negative numbers

Expressions & Equations

Move from arithmetic to working with variables and algebraic expressions.

  • Algebraic expressions with exponents
  • Writing expressions for real situations
  • Understand and solve simple equations
  • Represent inequalities

Geometry

Solve real-world problems involving area, surface area, and volume.

  • Area of triangles, quadrilaterals & polygons
  • Polygons on the coordinate plane
  • Volume of prisms (fractional edges)
  • Nets and surface area of 3D figures
